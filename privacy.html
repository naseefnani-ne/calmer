Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Calm Cloud is designed to support emotional regulation for children and families. We aim to keep data collection minimal.

What data is stored

Calm Cloud stores routine steps, progress counters, debrief notes, and optional parent PIN locally in your browser on your device.

What data is shared

By default, Calm Cloud does not send personal data to external servers.

Children's privacy

Caregivers are responsible for supervision and for deciding what to record in notes. Avoid entering sensitive personal identifiers in free-text fields.

Medical disclaimer

Calm Cloud is a supportive wellness tool and not a substitute for medical or mental health care.
