About Calm Cloud

Calm Cloud is a calming companion app built to help children with ADHD and hyperactivity regulate emotions, release energy safely, and return to focus.

Who it is for

Kids, caregivers, teachers, and support staff who want fast, practical regulation tools.

How to use

Start with Quick Calm, breathing, or movement-based options. Use check-ins and debrief logs to understand patterns over time.

Safety note

If a child is in danger or in severe distress, prioritize immediate safety and seek appropriate professional support.
